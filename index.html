About
WiDS

WiDS (WiDS DALLAS @UT Dallas) is an independent event organized by Drs. Latifur Khan and Bhavani Thuraisingham,
to coincide with the annual Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference held at Stanford University
and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS regional events,
which features outstanding women doing outstanding work.
